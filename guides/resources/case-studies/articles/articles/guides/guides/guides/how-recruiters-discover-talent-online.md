# How Recruiters Discover Talent Online

By Leo Zane Gouws

## Introduction

Recruitment has changed dramatically.

In the past, getting hired mostly depended on:

- qualifications
- CV submissions
- job applications
- referrals

Today, digital discoverability plays a major role in recruitment.

Recruiters increasingly search online before contacting candidates.

This means your online presence can influence whether opportunities find you or ignore you completely.

## Modern Recruitment Is Digital

Recruiters now use platforms like:

- LinkedIn
- Google Search
- AI-powered search systems
- professional databases
- social platforms

Before contacting candidates, recruiters often search for:

- expertise
- credibility
- authority
- consistency
- digital activity
- professional positioning

This changes employability completely.

## The Rise of Searchable Talent

The internet has become a career discovery system.

People who are easier to discover online often gain more opportunities.

This includes professionals who understand:

- LinkedIn optimization
- SEO
- personal branding
- digital visibility
- online positioning
- AI discoverability

Searchability is becoming a competitive advantage.

## Why LinkedIn Optimization Matters

LinkedIn is one of the biggest recruiter discovery platforms in the world.

Recruiters search LinkedIn using:

- keywords
- industries
- job titles
- skills
- expertise signals
- activity history

This means your LinkedIn profile should be optimized strategically.

## Important LinkedIn Elements

### Headline Optimization

Your headline should clearly communicate:

- expertise
- industry relevance
- discoverability keywords

Example:

SEO Specialist | AI Visibility | Digital Marketing South Africa

### About Section

Your About section should explain:

- who you are
- what you do
- your expertise
- your value
- your focus areas

### Content Activity

Posting consistently improves:

- visibility
- credibility
- authority
- discoverability
